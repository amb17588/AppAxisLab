1. Introduction

PeriodTracker ("the App") is a menstrual cycle and ovulation tracking application developed by App Axis Lab ("we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is our highest priority.

This policy explains what data the App handles, how it handles that data, and what rights you have. We've designed PeriodTracker so that your health data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to create an encrypted backup.

The short version: All your health data stays on your phone, encrypted. We don't have a server. We can't see your data. We don't sell it. We don't share it. Period.

2. Data We Process

Health data you enter

The App processes the following health-related data that you voluntarily enter:

  • Menstrual cycle dates (period start and end dates)
  • Flow intensity
  • Symptoms and their severity
  • Mood
  • Basal body temperature (BBT) readings
  • Cervical mucus observations
  • Sexual activity
  • Pill reminder status (taken, skipped, missed)

Profile preferences

  • Birth year (for age verification only — not your full date of birth)
  • Cycle and period length defaults
  • Temperature unit preference (°F / °C)
  • Goal mode (tracking, conceiving, or fertility awareness)

What we do NOT collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical address, location, IP address, device identifiers, or any data that could identify you personally.

3. Where Your Data Lives

All health data is stored exclusively on your device. It is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption (via SQLCipher). The encryption key is stored in your device's secure hardware (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) and is protected by your biometric authentication or device passcode.

We operate no backend servers. We maintain no database of user health data. We have no ability to access, read, or retrieve your health data.

Scenario Data leaves device? Encrypted?
Normal app usage No Encrypted at rest (AES-256)
Cloud backup (optional, paid) Yes — to your own cloud storage AES-256 encrypted before upload; we never hold the key
Data export (JSON/CSV) Only if you share it Your responsibility after export

4. What We Do Not Do

We want to be unambiguous:

  • We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third party for any purpose.
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not send your health data to any server we operate.
  • We do not create user profiles, behavioral models, or data aggregations.
  • We do not use meta-pixels, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar tracking technology.
  • We do not collect or process your geographic location.

5. Subscription Data

If you subscribe to PeriodTracker Premium, your payment is processed entirely by Apple (via the App Store) or Google (via Google Play). We never see your payment details (credit card number, billing address, etc.).

Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat, Inc., a third-party service that receives anonymous transaction data from Apple and Google. RevenueCat assigns a pseudonymous identifier (not linked to your identity) to track your subscription status. RevenueCat does not receive any health data from the App.

For RevenueCat's privacy practices, see revenuecat.com/privacy.

6. Cloud Backup (Optional, Paid Feature)

If you choose to use the Cloud Backup feature:

  • Your data is encrypted on your device using a password you set, before it leaves your device.
  • The encrypted backup is stored in your own cloud storage account (Google Drive, iCloud, OneDrive, Dropbox, or Box).
  • We never possess the encryption key and cannot decrypt your backup.
  • A recovery code generated during setup allows you to restore your data on a new device. If you lose both your device and your recovery code, your data is unrecoverable. We cannot help in this scenario — this is the privacy tradeoff.

Important: We do not operate the cloud storage. Your backup lives in your personal Google Drive, iCloud, OneDrive, Dropbox, or Box account. We have no access to it.

7. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the following rights in the App:

Delete

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data. This action is irreversible and immediate. All health data, preferences, and logs are permanently erased from your device.

Export

You can export your data in JSON or CSV format at any time from Settings → Data Management → Export Data. The exported file is in a standard format usable by other applications.

Access

All your data is visible to you within the App at all times. There is no hidden data collection.

For EU/EEA residents (GDPR)

You have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to data portability and the right to erasure. The App supports both via the Export and Delete features described above. Because we do not process your data on our servers, there is no data controller action required for most rights — your data is already under your control.

For California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell or share your personal information. You have the right to know what data is collected (this policy), the right to delete (in-app), and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (not applicable — we never sell data).

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions where GDPR applies). Age verification is performed during the App's onboarding process. We do not knowingly collect data from children below these ages.

9. Law Enforcement Requests

Because we do not operate servers or databases containing user data, we have no data to provide in response to subpoenas, court orders, or government requests.

Cloud backups are encrypted with user-held keys. We do not possess decryption keys and cannot comply with requests to decrypt backup data.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you via an in-app notification before the changes take effect. Continued use of the App after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Non-material changes (formatting, clarification, typo fixes) may be made without notification.
