ISmart Translator Voice and Text Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

ISmart Translator Voice and Text ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not require account creation.

If you contact us:

  • Email address
  • Any information you provide

b. Audio & Text Data

To provide translation:

  • Voice input (microphone)
  • Text entered by the user

Note:

  • Data is processed in real-time
  • We do not store this data permanently

c. Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device model
  • OS version
  • App version
  • Language settings

d. Usage Data

We may collect anonymized:

  • App usage
  • Crash logs
  • Feature interaction

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide translation services
  • Improve performance and UX
  • Fix bugs
  • Respond to support queries

3. Permissions

  • Microphone: For voice translation. Not stored.
  • Internet: Required for translation and ads.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads.

AdMob may collect and use:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data

This helps show personalized ads.

Personalized Ads

  • Ads may be tailored based on user interests
  • Users in certain regions (EEA, UK, etc.) may be asked for consent

Opt-Out

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Adjusting device ad settings
  • Visiting: https://adssettings.google.com

For more details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

We implement:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Minimal data collection
  • No unnecessary storage

7. Data Retention

  • Translation data is not stored
  • Emails may be retained for support

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If identified, data will be deleted.
